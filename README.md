# AdventureWorks Sales Analysis - Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end **Business Intelligence dashboard** built using **Power BI** on the AdventureWorks dataset.  
The report provides actionable insights into **revenue, profit, orders, customers, products, and geographic performance** through interactive and well-structured visuals.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  
- DAX (Measures & KPIs)  

---

## Dataset Information
- Dataset: AdventureWorks  
- Time Period: 2020 – 2022  
- Currency: INR (₹)  
- Data Domains: Sales, Products, Customers, Geography  

---

## Report Pages & Analysis

### Dashboard
**Key KPIs**
- Total Revenue: ₹24.9M  
- Total Profit: ₹10.5M  
- Total Orders: 25.2K  
- Return Rate: 2.2%  

**Visuals**
- Revenue trend with forecast
- Orders by product category
- Top 10 products by orders and revenue
- Monthly revenue, orders, and returns
- Most ordered and most returned products

**Purpose:**  
High-level performance monitoring for business stakeholders.

---

### Product Details
**Features**
- Product-level performance analysis
- Monthly Orders vs Target (Gauge)
- Monthly Revenue vs Target (Gauge)
- Dynamic metric selector (Orders, Revenue, Profit, Returns, Return Rate)
- Price adjustment slider to simulate pricing impact
- Monthly performance trends

**Purpose:**  
Analyze individual product performance and profitability.

---

### Customer Details
**KPIs**
- Unique Customers: 2,422  
- Average Revenue per Customer: ₹773.82  

**Visuals**
- Customer trend over time
- Top 100 customers by revenue
- Orders by income level
- Orders by occupation
- Highlight of top customer by revenue

**Purpose:**  
Customer segmentation and high-value customer identification.

---

### Location Analysis
**Features**
- Interactive world map
- Region-based filters:
  - Europe
  - North America
  - Pacific

**Purpose:**  
Geographical performance analysis and regional comparison.

---

## Data Preparation & Modeling
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
- Relationship modeling between fact and dimension tables
- Creation of calculated measures using DAX
- Cross-filtering and page-level interactions enabled

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows steady growth from 2020 to 2022
- Accessories and Bikes are top-performing categories
- A small group of customers contributes significant revenue
- Return rate remains consistently low
- Regional sales performance varies across markets
